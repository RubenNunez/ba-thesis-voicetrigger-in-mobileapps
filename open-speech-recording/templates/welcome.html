FOOBY Speech Recording

As part of my bachelor's thesis at HSLU, focusing on the integration of voice control features in mobile apps, collecting voice samples is of essential importance. These samples will assist in recognizing a trigger word or a sequence of trigger words in spoken language, ultimately enhancing the user experience considerably.
This project is a fork and adaptation of the open-speech-recording repository from Pete Warden on GitHub, adjusted for my specific use case.
Before you proceed, please review and confirm the following agreements:
Participation Agreement: By participating, you agree that your voice recordings will be used exclusively for my bachelor's thesis at HSLU and for the use case of the FOOBY app. The recordings will be treated confidentially and will not be made publicly available.
